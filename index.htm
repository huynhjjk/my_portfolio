About

I am currently majoring in Human Centered Design & Engineering (HCDE) with an emphasis in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) at the University of Washington. I chose HCDE because I have always had an extreme interest in the metamorphosis of technology and a passion for easing the lives of people. Since technology has now evolved toward a more user-based interaction, I want to help people have a smoother and more efficient experience utilizing computers and technical devices. The appeal of the HCDE field lies in its interdisciplinary approach: by combining aspects of computer science, psychology, industrial design, and other various fields, this approach not only allows me to learn about a variety of subjects at once, but also synthesizes them into a purposeful whole. HCDE provides countless opportunities to learn and gain experience in a field that is rapidly becoming a critical component to the technological world.

As an emerging front-end developer that focuses on the user experience, I will contribute my drive to succeed and my determination to serve people’s needs. Through my academic work, I have accumulated experience in both researching and designing. I have come to understand the processes and goals that need to be met in order to achieve maximal success. The research I have conducted includes administering interviews, constructing surveys, performing field studies, and generating personas in order to discover key solutions to improve the design of current nutrition labels. In terms of designing, I have created a web application that serves a dual-purpose for the convenience of the user: it not only provides directions for the user, but also notifies them of the weather conditions that surround their planned destination.

Whether I am working independently or cooperating with others, I will ensure a productive development within a reasonable timeframe. The knowledge, skills, and experiences I have gained from HCDE and my work are connected because they have prepared me for a career in web and mobile application design and development. I now have a strong understanding of the kinds of methods, procedures, and responsibilities I need to take in order to meet user expectations. By conducting research, writing documentations, and designing applications and webpages, I have come to realize the usefulness of incorporating valuable research findings and ideas into the design of user-friendly products. As a result, I am able to understand not only what a user-friendly design is, but also how the research process can be incorporated in order to become an effective and efficient front-end developer that focuses on the user experience.
